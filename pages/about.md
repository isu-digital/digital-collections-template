## About the collection

This digital collection contains photographs of Works Progress Administration, later Work Projects Administration (WPA), projects done in Iowa. Material relating to the Iowa WPA, including brochures, statistical graphs, and flyers are also included. Note: Three photographs containing racist imagery (blackface) were not included in the digital collection. The physical items, in box 7, folder 3, can be seen in person in the Special Collections and University Archives reading room, or by contacting [archives@iastate.edu](mailto:archives@iastate.edu) to request individual digital copies.
